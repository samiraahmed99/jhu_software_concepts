Samira Ahmed

Master's Student in Data Science at Johns Hopkins University

Samira Ahmed is currently pursuing a Master’s in Data Science through the Johns Hopkins Engineering for Professionals program. Her goal is to enhance her practical skill set and apply data-driven solutions to real-world problems, bridging the gap between theory and application.

She holds a Bachelor’s degree in Data Theory from the University of California, Los Angeles. During her undergraduate years, Samira developed a passion for working with data — especially after competing in UCLA’s two-day hackathon, DataFest, where her team received the Judge's Choice Award. That experience solidified her interest in applied data science.

Since then, Samira has focused on gaining hands-on experience with increasingly large and complex datasets. Her graduate studies allow her to deepen that foundation and sharpen her ability to solve problems at scale using modern tools and methods.
